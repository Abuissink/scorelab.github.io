SCoRe Lab studies and produces sustainable computing solutions with respect to low cost computing and communication foundations in developing and emerging regions in the world. We have developed several affordable and sustainable ICT solutions specially focused on the requirements of these regions. These solutions are briefly described in the projects section.

As the maintainer of several open-source projects, we would love your help with these projects. Go to our Projects Page to view our projects, check out our GitHub or fill in the form below and someone will get back to you. Alternatively, if you are a student you may be able to contribute through Google Summer of Code or Google Code-In
